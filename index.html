How to work?

1. Publish/broadcast the transaction information

2. Miners compete to take the right to add the records to the open ledger by validating and calculating the key.

3. Miner who wins the competition adds the records to its own ledger and broadcast the solution to the network. The miner will get the reward.

4. How synchonize? Other miners find that one block has been added and link it to its own ledger by using the published solution. Then give up calculating and look for next unvalidated transactions.
